<p><p>The data storage market encompasses various types of storage systems tailored to different performance and capacity needs. All-Flash Arrays utilize solid-state drives for high-speed access and low latency, making them ideal for demanding applications. Hybrid Storage Arrays combine flash and traditional hard disk drives (HDDs) to balance cost, performance, and capacity, offering a versatile solution for mixed workloads. HDD Arrays primarily rely on mechanical drives, providing high capacity at lower costs, suitable for archival and less performance-intensive storage needs.</p></p>
