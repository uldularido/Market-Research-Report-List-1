<p><ul><li>Fixed Wings</li><li>Rotary Wings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hybrid airship market consists of two main types: fixed wings and rotary wings. Fixed-wing hybrid airships combine characteristics of traditional airplanes and airships, offering efficient long-distance travel with the capability to land on various terrains. In contrast, rotary-wing hybrid airships, akin to helicopters, emphasize vertical takeoff and landing (VTOL) capabilities, making them suited for urban environments and inaccessible areas. Both types leverage buoyancy and advanced aerodynamics for enhanced fuel efficiency and operational versatility in various applications.</p></p>
