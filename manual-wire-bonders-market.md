<p><p>Manual wire bonders are specialized equipment used in semiconductor packaging to create electrical connections between microchips and substrates. The market for these bonders includes three main types: Ball-Wedge manual wire bonders, which use a ball bond technique for connections; Wedge-Wedge bonders, which employ a wedge bonding method for enhanced performance in certain materials; and Convertible manual wire bonders, designed to switch between ball and wedge bonding modes for versatility in applications. Each type caters to specific industry needs and material compatibility.</p></p>
