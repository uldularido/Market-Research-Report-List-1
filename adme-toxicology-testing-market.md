<p><p>ADME-Toxicology testing involves various market types that assess how substances interact within biological systems. In-vitro testing utilizes isolated cells or biological molecules to evaluate toxicity and absorption. Biochemical testing focuses on interactions at the molecular level, determining how compounds affect biological processes. Cell testing examines the effects of substances on live cells to observe physiological responses. In-vivo testing involves whole organisms to assess the overall impact of substances, providing data on effectiveness and safety in real biological environments.</p></p>
