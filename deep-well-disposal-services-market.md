<p><p>The Deep-Well Disposal Services market encompasses various waste types including solids, sludges, leachate, ammonia, and biological/chemical oxygen demand (BOD & COD) materials. Solids refer to any non-liquid waste that requires specialized handling. Sludges are semi-solid residues often from wastewater treatment. Leachate consists of contaminated liquid that drains from landfills. Ammonia is a nitrogenous compound needing careful disposal due to its toxicity. BOD and COD materials indicate organic matter levels in wastewater, demanding treatment to mitigate environmental impact.</p></p>
