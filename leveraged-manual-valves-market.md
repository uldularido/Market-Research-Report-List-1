<p><p>The Leveraged Manual Valves market encompasses various types, each serving specific applications. Gate valves are designed for on/off control with minimal pressure drop, while cock valves offer quick shut-off with a quarter-turn mechanism. Butterfly valves utilize a rotating disc to manage flow, providing efficiency in larger pipelines. Other valves include globe valves, check valves, and ball valves, each with unique functionalities for fluid regulation. Together, these valves facilitate precise control and management of flow in various industrial applications.</p></p>
