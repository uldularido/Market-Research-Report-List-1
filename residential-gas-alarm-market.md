<p><p>The residential gas alarm market is divided into two main types: stationary gas alarms and portable gas alarms. They typically feature advanced detection technology and are often connected to home security systems. Portable gas alarms, on the other hand, are handheld devices that can be easily moved to different locations as needed, offering flexibility for temporary monitoring in various settings.</p></p>
