<p><p>The Hydrogen Peroxide Sterilization Equipment market comprises two main types: Vaporized Hydrogen Peroxide (VHP) sterilization equipment and Fogging Hydrogen Peroxide sterilization equipment. VHP systems utilize vaporization to create a mist of hydrogen peroxide, effectively penetrating surfaces and effectively eliminating pathogens in enclosed spaces. Fogging equipment, on the other hand, disperses hydrogen peroxide in a fine fog, allowing for broader coverage and effective sterilization in larger areas. Both methods are widely used in healthcare, pharmaceuticals, and cleanroom environments for maintaining sterility.</p></p>
