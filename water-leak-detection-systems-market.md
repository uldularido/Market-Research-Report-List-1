<p><strong>The Water Leak Detection Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Leak Detection Systems</li><li>Active Leak Detection Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Water leak detection systems are categorized into passive and active types. Passive leak detection systems identify leaks by monitoring environmental changes, such as moisture levels or humidity, alerting users when thresholds are exceeded. These systems are often simpler and can provide early warnings. In contrast, active leak detection systems rely on continuous monitoring using sensors to detect leaks and automatically shut off water supply to prevent damage. These systems offer more proactive protection and are essential in high-risk areas.</p></p>
