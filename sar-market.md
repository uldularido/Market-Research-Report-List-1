<p><p>The SAR market is primarily divided into space-based and airborne systems. Space-based SAR utilizes satellites equipped with synthetic aperture radar to capture large-scale earth imagery, offering global coverage and aiding applications like disaster management and climate monitoring. In contrast, airborne SAR systems are mounted on aircraft, providing high-resolution data for more localized assessments, such as urban planning and agricultural monitoring. Both types play crucial roles in various sectors, including defense, environmental monitoring, and infrastructure development.</p></p>
