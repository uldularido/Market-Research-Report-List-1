<p><p>The Motorcycle Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU) Sensors market is primarily categorized into two types: Attitude and Heading Reference System (AHRS) and Inertial Navigation System (INS). AHRS provides critical information about the motorcycle's orientation, helping enhance stability and control during rides. In contrast, INS offers precise positioning data by integrating acceleration and rotation measurements, enabling advanced navigation capabilities. Both systems are essential for improving rider safety, performance, and overall riding experience in modern motorcycles.</p></p>
