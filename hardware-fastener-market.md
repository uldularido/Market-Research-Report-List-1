<p><p>The hardware and fastener market encompasses various essential components used in construction and manufacturing. Nuts are hexagonal fasteners that secure bolts, while bolts are threaded rods that hold materials together. Rivets are permanent fasteners that join materials by deforming the tail end, providing strong, lasting bonds. Locks are mechanisms for securing objects against unauthorized access, and other fasteners can include screws, anchors, and clips, each serving specific functions to ensure stability and safety in various applications.</p></p>
