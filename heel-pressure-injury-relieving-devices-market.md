<p><strong>The Heel Pressure Injury Relieving Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Constant Low Pressure Devices</li><li>Alternating Pressure Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Heel pressure injury relieving devices are designed to reduce pressure on the heels and prevent ulcers in at-risk patients. Constant low pressure devices maintain a stable and minimal pressure over the heel, dispersing weight effectively to prevent tissue damage. In contrast, alternating pressure devices use cyclical changes in pressure to promote blood circulation and alleviate pressure points. Both types play a crucial role in healthcare settings, particularly for individuals with limited mobility, enhancing patient comfort and reducing the risk of injury.</p></p>
