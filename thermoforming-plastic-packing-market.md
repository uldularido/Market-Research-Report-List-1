<p><strong>The Thermoforming Plastic Packing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vacuum Formed</li><li>Pressure Formed</li><li>Mechanical Formed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermoforming plastic packaging market encompasses three primary types: vacuum formed, pressure formed, and mechanically formed packaging. </p><p>Vacuum forming involves heating plastic sheets and creating a mold by drawing the heated sheet over it, producing lightweight and cost-effective packaging. </p><p>Pressure forming enhances this process by using pressurized air for more intricate designs and better detail. </p><p>Mechanical forming utilizes machinery to shape plastic sheets, allowing for high-speed production and uniformity, making it suitable for a wide range of applications in packaging.</p></p>
