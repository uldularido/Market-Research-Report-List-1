DC smart chargers directly convert and distribute direct current, allowing for faster charging of electric vehicles and facilitating high power output. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing charging efficiency, grid interaction, and user convenience, supporting the growing electric vehicle ecosystem.</p></p>
