<p><ul><li>Double Coated Tape</li><li>Single Coated Tape</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The adhesive cloth tape market is primarily divided into two types: double-coated tape and single-coated tape. Double-coated tape features adhesive on both sides, making it ideal for applications requiring bonding two surfaces together, such as mounting and splicing. In contrast, single-coated tape has adhesive on only one side, suited for general-purpose uses like binding, sealing, and reinforcement. Both types cater to diverse industries including automotive, construction, and electronics, ensuring effective adhesion and durability in various applications.</p></p>
