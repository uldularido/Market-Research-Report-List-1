Irrigation cannulae facilitate the flushing or cleansing of the surgical site, ensuring visibility and reducing infection risks. The "others" category includes specialized cannulae for specific applications like aspirating or introducing gases. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing surgical efficiency and patient outcomes in laparoscopic procedures.</p></p>
