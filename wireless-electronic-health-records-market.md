<p><p>The Wireless Electronic Health Records market encompasses various communication technologies for transmitting patient data. 3G and commercial cellular networks offer wide coverage for remote accessibility. Sensors monitor patient conditions wirelessly, and WiMAX supports long-range high-speed internet. Wireless LANs enable local networking in healthcare facilities, and Zigbee is ideal for low-power, short-range applications, particularly in home health monitoring.</p></p>
