<p><p>The BOPP (Bi-oriented Polypropylene) market comprises various types, including metal oxides coated and metal coated types. Metal oxides coated BOPP films are enhanced with thin layers of metal oxides that improve barrier properties, providing resistance to moisture and gases, making them suitable for packaging applications. In contrast, metal coated BOPP films involve a metallic layer that offers reflective properties and enhanced aesthetics, often used in decorative applications and packaging that requires high visibility and product protection.</p></p>
