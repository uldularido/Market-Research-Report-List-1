<p><strong>The Enterprise Infrastructure Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Public</li><li>Private & Hybrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Enterprise infrastructure markets are categorized into three types: public, private, and hybrid. Public infrastructure is based on cloud services offered by third-party providers, allowing multiple organizations to share resources, enhancing scalability and cost-effectiveness. Private infrastructure is dedicated to a single organization, ensuring more control, security, and customized solutions, often preferred for sensitive data. Hybrid infrastructure combines both public and private elements, enabling flexibility by allowing data and applications to move between environments, optimizing performance and resource utilization.</p></p>
