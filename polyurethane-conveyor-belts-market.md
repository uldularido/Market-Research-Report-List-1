TPU conveyor belts are known for their superior elasticity, abrasion resistance, and flexibility, making them suitable for various industrial applications. PUR conveyor belts offer excellent chemical resistance and are ideal for handling food products and bulk materials. Both types are lightweight, durable, and offer high performance across diverse environments, contributing to their growing demand in sectors like manufacturing, logistics, and food processing.</p></p>
