<p><p>The Electronics Access Control System (EACS) encompasses various market types including Access Controllers, Card Readers, Electric Control Locks, and Others. Access Controllers manage and regulate user permissions, ensuring secure entry points. Card Readers process identification credentials, granting or denying access based on authorized data. Electric Control Locks provide physical security by electronically locking or unlocking doors, integrating seamlessly with the overall system.
