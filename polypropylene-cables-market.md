<p><p>The Polypropylene Cables market can be categorized into two main types: Multi-Mode and Single-Mode cables. Multi-Mode cables are designed for short-distance transmission, utilizing multiple light paths for data transfer, making them suitable for local area networks and multimedia applications. In contrast, Single-Mode cables are optimized for long-distance communication, using a single light path to reduce signal attenuation and allow for higher bandwidth over greater distances. Both types serve distinct networking needs and industries.</p></p>
