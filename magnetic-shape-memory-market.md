<p><p>The Magnetic Shape Memory market is categorized into three main types. The one-way memory effect involves materials that change shape in response to a magnetic field, returning to their original form when the field is removed. The two-way memory effect allows materials to remember two distinct shapes, switching between them with the application of a magnetic field and retaining one shape when the field is removed. Other market types may include variations or advanced applications of these effects in specific industries, enhancing functionality and performance.</p></p>
