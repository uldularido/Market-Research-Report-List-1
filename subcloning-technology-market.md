<p><p>The subcloning technology market comprises two primary types: cell cloning and molecular cloning. Cell cloning refers to the replication of specific cells or cell lines for research, therapeutic, or industrial applications, focusing on the proliferation of individual cells. Molecular cloning, on the other hand, involves creating copies of specific DNA fragments, allowing for the manipulation and study of genes. Both types are crucial for advancements in biotechnology, genomics, and medicine, facilitating research and development in various fields.</p></p>
