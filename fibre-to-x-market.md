<p><p>Fibre to X (FTTx) encompasses various deployment strategies of fiber optic technology. Fiber to Buildings (FTTB) connects fiber directly to multiple tenants in a building, enhancing broadband access. Fiber to Desks (FTTD) delivers fiber directly to individual workspaces, ensuring high-speed internet for businesses. Fiber to Nodes (FTTN) brings fiber to a neighborhood node, using copper for last-mile connectivity, suitable for widespread areas. Other types include Fiber to Home (FTTH) and Fiber to the Curb (FTTC), catering to diverse user needs and infrastructure capabilities.</p></p>
