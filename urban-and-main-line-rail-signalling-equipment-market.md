<p><p>The Urban and Main Line Rail Signalling Equipment Market comprises various systems that enhance train safety and efficiency. PTC (Positive Train Control), ETCS (European Train Control System), and CTCS (Chinese Train Control System) are advanced methods ensuring real-time monitoring and control. Basic CBTC (Communications-Based Train Control) allows for automated train operations in urban settings. The FAO (Fixed Automatic Block) system provides traditional block signalling, while I-CBTC (Integrated CBTC) integrates multiple technologies for optimized urban transit, improving train frequency and safety.</p></p>
