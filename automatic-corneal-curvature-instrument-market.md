<p><ul><li>Helmholtz Type</li><li>Javal-Schiotz Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Corneal Curvature Instrument market features various types, including the Helmholtz Type and the Javal-Schiotz Type. The Helmholtz Type employs a system of mirrors to accurately measure corneal curvature, providing precise data for refractive assessments. In contrast, the Javal-Schiotz Type utilizes a more mechanical approach with weighted probes to evaluate corneal topography. Both types serve essential roles in ophthalmic diagnostics, catering to different clinical needs and preferences in measuring corneal curvature for effective vision correction outcomes.</p></p>
