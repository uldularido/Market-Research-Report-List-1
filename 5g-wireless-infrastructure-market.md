<p><p>The 5G wireless infrastructure market comprises several key components. Communication towers are essential for transmitting signals over long distances. Transmitting antennas convert electrical signals into radio waves, enabling data transmission. Receiving antennas capture these radio waves and convert them back into electrical signals for processing. Decoders play a crucial role in interpreting the received data, ensuring that information is accurately translated for user devices. Together, these elements form a robust ecosystem that supports high-speed, reliable 5G connectivity.</p></p>
