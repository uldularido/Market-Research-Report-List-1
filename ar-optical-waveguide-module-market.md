Diffractive waveguides employ microstructures to manipulate light through diffraction, allowing for compact designs and enhanced integration. Other types may involve hybrid technologies or unique materials tailored for specific applications, expanding the functionality and versatility of augmented reality systems. Each type contributes to the overall performance and user experience in AR devices.</p></p>
