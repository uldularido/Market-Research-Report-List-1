<p><strong>The Data Desensitization Technologies Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dynamic Masking</li><li>Static Masking</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Data desensitization technologies are crucial for protecting sensitive information while retaining data utility. The market is primarily segmented into dynamic masking and static masking. Dynamic masking allows real-time data protection by altering data as it is accessed, ensuring that sensitive information is obscured during operations, while original data remains intact. In contrast, static masking involves the permanent alteration of data, creating a non-sensitive version for use in testing or analytics, thus minimizing the risk of data breaches.</p></p>
