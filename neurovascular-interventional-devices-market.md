<p><p>The neurovascular interventional devices market encompasses various products used in treating vascular conditions in the brain. Embolization coils are used to occlude blood vessels, while carotid stents support narrowed carotid arteries. Intracranial stents maintain vessel patency, and flow diverters redirect blood flow to promote aneurysm healing. Intrasaccular devices function within aneurysms to facilitate their occlusion. Other devices may include balloons or guide wires that assist in neurovascular procedures, each playing a crucial role in enhancing patient outcomes in neurointervention.</p></p>
